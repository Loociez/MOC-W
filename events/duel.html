Duels

Overview

Duels are a 1v1 combat activity where two players face off in a controlled arena environment.

To begin a duel, both players must stand on the designated dueling pads and interact with the Duels Master NPC.

Once initiated, the duel begins and the objective is simple — defeat your opponent by reducing their HP to 0.

Duels Arena

How To Start

Starting a duel requires cooperation between two players:

  • Both players must stand on the dueling pads
  • Either player (or anyone within the arena map) can interact with the Duels Master NPC
  • The duel will begin once initiated

Ensure both players are ready before starting, as the duel begins immediately after activation.

Objective

The goal of a duel is to defeat your opponent in a fair 1v1 fight.

Victory is achieved by reducing your opponent's HP to 0.

Etiquette

Duels are governed by an unspoken community rule:

Players are expected not to use HP potions during a duel.

This is considered a "gentleman's agreement" and breaking it is heavily frowned upon by other players.

While not enforced by the game, respecting this rule maintains fairness and sportsmanship.

Rewards

Winning a duel rewards you with Dueling Skill Experience.

This contributes toward your overall Dueling Level.

The Dueling skill has no effect on gameplay or other skills and serves purely as a way to showcase your experience and achievements.

Tips

Make sure both players agree on rules before starting — especially regarding potion use.

Timing your abilities correctly can be the difference between victory and defeat.

Learn your opponent’s playstyle and adapt during the fight.

Practice regularly to improve your reaction time and decision making.

Stay calm under pressure — panic often leads to mistakes.
