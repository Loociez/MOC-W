Ice Sliding Puzzle

Overview

The Ice Sliding Puzzle is a traversal-based minigame where players must navigate across slippery ice tiles to reach the end goal.

Movement is uncontrollable once started on ice — players will continue sliding until they hit a stopping point or obstacle.

The objective is to successfully reach the far end of the puzzle and open the reward chest.

How It Works

The puzzle is designed around momentum-based movement:

  • Step onto ice tiles to begin sliding
  • You will continue moving in a direction until stopped
  • Plan your route carefully to avoid sliding past safe paths

The challenge comes from controlling direction and timing movement across the icy terrain.

Objective

Reach the end of the ice path without falling into incorrect routes or getting stuck.

Once you reach the final area, interact with the chest to claim your reward.

Reward

Inside the chest lies a reward of X.

This reward is granted upon successfully completing the puzzle and opening the chest at the end.
