/PvP

Overview

/PvP is a guild-based free-for-all combat event where players compete against each other for control and rewards.

This event allows players to freely engage in combat with others, making it a fast-paced and competitive environment.

The goal is to build progress toward 100% and secure victory.

PvP Combat Area

Objective Boss

Before players can begin capturing the point, they must first defeat the event enemy Darwin.

Name: Darwin

HP: 6000

Physical Attack: 590

Defense: 0

Mechanic: Must be defeated before the capture point becomes active.

How It Works

The /pvp event is a Free-For-All (FFA) where players fight against each other to gain progress.

As players participate and dominate the event, progress builds toward 100%.

Once a player reaches 100, they are rewarded with:

  • Victory Token

This token can be used for:

  • 10,000 Guild Experience
  • 1,000,000 Player Experience

Drop Mechanics

This event takes place in a "Drops" enabled area.

If you die during the event, you will lose:

  • A random currency note value OR
  • A random item from your inventory

This adds risk to every fight and encourages careful engagement.

Tips

Defeating Darwin quickly gives a huge advantage — be ready for PvP immediately after.

Stay aware of your surroundings — multiple players can attack at once.

Pick your fights wisely to avoid unnecessary deaths and item loss.

Keep track of your inventory — bring only what you're willing to lose.

Timing and positioning are key to securing the final push to 100%.
