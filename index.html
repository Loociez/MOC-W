Unofficial Mirage Online Classic Wiki

Welcome

Welcome to my unofficial Mirage Online Classic Wiki.

This is an effort to gather atleast a "complete" wiki, obviously details will always change as updates happen. All updates will be published by myself (Loocie), This is still for the community to use if they so wish, Think of this as an extension of the official Wiki.

This is a work in progress, and as such should be treated with a grain of salt, I update this when i have free time between my many other projects. If something is incorrect please feel free to message me, or even if you wish to provide extra information, images and the like.

Where the data is pulled from:

As a team effort i cannot say this is soley my work, I have to give credit where credit is due and without the communities support, images, information and wiki entires from the official wikis over the years.

This would not have been possible. There are however way to many people to credit and to be honest some of the information no longer has sources linked to them or since been lost in the depth of the internet. So all i can do is say thank you to any of the information you provided that ended up here.

Guides, dungeon walkthroughs, and detailed mechanics will be added over time.
