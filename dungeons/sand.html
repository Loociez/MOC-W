A Shoe Full of Sand

Overview

A Shoe Full of Sand

Description: No Description added.

Location: South of North Coast Village

Difficulty: Hard

Reward: Sand

A Shoe Full of Sand is a puzzle-based dungeon with no NPC enemies until completion. The objective is to complete two maze floors before receiving your reward.

Tasks

1: Complete the 2 mazes.

2: Collect the Sand.

NPCs You May Encounter

There are no enemies present during the dungeon itself. A single mob appears only after completing the dungeon when preparing to exit.

Lesser NPC

Name:Desert Wraith

HP: 2250

Attack: 700

Defense:PDef: 10, RDef: 15 and MDef: 15

Boss Mechanic:Nothing

May Drop:Nothing

Maze Mechanics

The maze alternates between two different tile layouts on the same map, creating confusion as the floor visually shifts between states.

Completion

Once both maze floors are completed, you are transported to a reward room.

Here you can collect your reward and exit via the blue orb.
