Nightmare Garden of the Wanderer

Overview

Nightmare Garden of the Wanderer

Description: "A corrupted version of the once peaceful garden, now overrun with hostile creatures."

Location: Northeast of Crendale

Difficulty: Advanced

Reward: High-tier Equipment, Experience & Rare Materials

Notes: Using "Pots of honey" to heal will aggro all bee's within a room, also standing on flowers will spawn them.

This advanced dungeon challenges players with complex navigation, dangerous enemies, and hidden secrets. Expect tougher encounters and rewarding loot for those who can survive.

Tasks

1: Enter the garden and take the alternate warp path.

2: Make your way through the flower maze.

3: Navigate through the infested bee house.

4: Find your way through the basement and burrows.

5: Enter the Queen's hive and defeat her.

6: Claim your rewards.

Bonus: Discover hidden secrets within the dungeon.
