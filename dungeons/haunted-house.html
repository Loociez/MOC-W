Into the Haunted House (Normal)

Description: Explore the Haunted House.

Location: Moderately West of Crendale

Difficulty: Easy

Reward:

This is a great early dungeon, it provides a decent challenge with having to use more mechanics like door opening, portals, mini boss fights and healing mobs.

Tasks

1: Slay the librarian to reveal the entrance... or find another way.

2: Slay Jasper after passing through the petrified forest and through the morgue.

3: Manoeuvre past the Gatekeepers and other hellish Demons.

4: Win the final battle to collect your reward.
