Nightmare Haunted House

Overview

Nightmare Haunted House

Description: None

Location: West of Crendale / Inside the Haunted House

Difficulty: Hard

Reward: Class Equipment & Experience

One of the newer dungeon additions, Nightmare Haunted House provides a fast-paced but difficult challenge aimed at higher level players.

Designed for Level 60 classes, this dungeon rewards powerful PvP-focused equipment and valuable experience.

While challenging, it can be completed solo or with a group depending on player skill and preparation. You will find many pathways, Some behind bookcases, others behind doors or warps.



Tasks

1: Venture into the Haunted House.

2: Find the entrance to Nightmare HH.

3: Venture through the dungeon.

4: Progress through the garden.

5: Progress through "crazy stairs".

6: Slay the final boss and his minions.

Bonus: Discover any secrets inside.
