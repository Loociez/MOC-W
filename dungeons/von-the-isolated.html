Overview

Von the Isolated

Description: "A lone, relentless entity roaming the wastes, seeking out any who dare challenge it."

Location: South-East of Draconis Wastes

Difficulty: Challenging

Reward: Experience, Von the Isolated Chest

This is a single-room boss encounter focused entirely on defeating a powerful enemy.

The fight is straightforward in design but requires strong positioning and awareness due to constant pressure from the boss.
