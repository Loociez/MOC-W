DMC - Dragon Mouth's Consortium - Normal

Description: "After all, wheres there's a cave theres a Dragon. right?"

Location: Dragon’s Mouth Cave

Difficulty: Beginner

Reward: Level 10 Equipment & Experience

This beginner boss fight dungeon, this will teach the player about boss mechanics and their alerts.

Tasks

1: Navigate through the Dragon mouth caves to find the beasts lair.

2: Slay the Beast.
