Garden of the Wanderer

Overview

Garden of the Wanderer - Normal

Description: "A mysterious garden hidden beneath the forest canopy."

Location: Darkcone Forest; Northeast of Crendale

Difficulty: Beginner

Reward: Level 2 Equipment & Experience

This beginner dungeon introduces players to more complex maze navigation and combat against multiple foes. A perfect way to earn early-game equipment and experience.

Tasks

1: Defeat the two horticulturalists to gain entry into the garden.

2: Avoid Angry Hipsters as you venture through the underground.

3: Climb the tree and make your way through the branch maze.

4: Exit the tangled root maze.

5: Make your way through the trees and claim your reward.
