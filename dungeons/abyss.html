Into the Abyss

Overview

Into the Abyss

Description: Journey through the depths of the Abyss

Location: Far north of Crendale

Difficulty: Challenging

Reward: +500 Max HP Potion and Experience

Into the Abyss is a challenging dungeon that tests players as they descend deeper into dangerous territory filled with powerful enemies.

Note: Walking accidently into the "Abyss" wave tiles will respawn you at the beginning of the entrance.

Tasks

1: Traverse through the Abyss.

2: Defeat enemies and survive the depths.

3: Defeat the boss and claim your reward.
