Down in the Well

Overview

Down in the Well - Normal

Description: "What could be down here?"

Location: Crendale

Difficulty: Very Easy

Reward: Level 1 Equipment & Experience

This is the first dungeon players will encounter. Very easy and designed to teach basic mechanics. It is also great for obtaining Beginner Skill Kits.

Task

1: Venture through the maze.

2: Slay the beast that awaits.

3: Find the true resting place of the fallen hero.
