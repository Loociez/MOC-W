Ability / Skill

Poison Strike

Assassin Using Poison

The Assassin cloaks, creating a cyclone effect, and becomes invisible for 2.5 seconds.

Upon reappearing, they strike their opponent, applying a poison damage over time effect.

This ability is similar to Samurai's counterattack, but instead of needing to be hit first, the Assassin initiates the attack to trigger the effect.
