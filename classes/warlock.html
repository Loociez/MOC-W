Ability / Skill

AOE Attack: Cast an AOE on your target selected with the interact button. Damage is 100% of your minimum MAT with an additional 2-second cooldown if targeting yourself or 4 seconds when focusing/targeting a foe.

Attack on all. Usually an explosion, can be shaped like a plus for Warlock.

A damage type: Magic Damage. Can be defended with MDF.

This ability costs 30 MP per AOE.
