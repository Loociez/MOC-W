Enchanting

Overview

Enchanting is a crafting skill in Mirage Online Classic that allows players to enhance their equipment using magical essences obtained through disenchanting items.

As your Enchanting level increases, stronger and more varied enchantments become available.

Enchanting activities are primarily performed in Alephvale, located east of Crendale Town.

Enchanting in Alephvale

Location

Alephvale – East of Crendale Town

How Enchanting Works

Enchanting consists of two core actions:

  • Disenchanting items to obtain essences and experience
  • Enchanting equipment using essences, runes, and gold

Progression unlocks stronger enchantments and additional recipe tiers.

Disenchanting

Warning: Disenchanting permanently destroys the item.

Disenchanting provides:

  • Essences (based on item level)
  • Enchanting experience

Higher-level items yield higher-tier essences.

Methods of Disenchanting

1. Character Menu

  • Equip the item
  • Right-click your character
  • Select Disenchant
  • Choose the equipment slot

This method is slower for bulk use.

2. Enchanter’s Circle (Recommended)

  • Located inside the Enchanter’s house in Alephvale
  • Drop items into the white circle
  • Items are instantly disenchanted

This is the fastest and most efficient method for large quantities.

Enchanting Items

Once essences are obtained, they can be used to enchant equipment using runes and gold.

Enchanting Progression

Tier Requirement Details
Tier 1 Level 1 Basic Defense enchantments
Tier 2 Level 15 Stronger enchantments unlocked
Tier 3 Level 25 Advanced enchantments including Attack Damage

Enchanting Costs

The cost of enchanting depends on:

  • The rune tier used
  • The strength of the enchantment

Higher-tier enchantments require more valuable materials and more gold.

Notes

  • Best leveled by disenchanting unused or low-value gear
  • Higher-level items give better essence and Enchanting experience yields
  • Enchanter’s Circle is highly recommended for efficiency
